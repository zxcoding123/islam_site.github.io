What is DeenConnect about?

DeenConnect is a platform designed to connect Muslims around Zamboanga City with a focus on religious education, community, and daily guidance. Our mission is to empower individuals to strengthen their faith and connect with like-minded people in a meaningful way. Join us as we cultivate spiritual growth, community engagement, and knowledge sharing.

Different Programs Offered by DeenConnect in Zamboanga City

Various Islamic Studies and Research Centers

Quran Education

The Quran is the primary source of Islamic knowledge and guidance. Studying the Quran helps deepen your faith and understanding of the teachings of Islam.

Hadith Studies

The sayings and actions of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) provide valuable lessons and guidance in the practice of Islam, with Hadith studies helping to solidify one’s understanding.

Fiqh (Islamic Jurisprudence)

Fiqh is the study of Islamic law and ethics, guiding Muslims in matters of worship, daily life, and community. It is vital for understanding religious obligations and rights.

Tafsir (Quranic Exegesis)

Tafsir provides deep insights and interpretation of the Quran’s verses, helping Muslims understand their meanings and implications in our everyday lives.

Islamic History

Islamic History covers the remarkable journey of Islam, from the life of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) to the flourishing of Muslim civilizations and their contributions to the world.

Islamic Philosophy

Islamic Philosophy explores the fundamental questions of existence, ethics, and human nature, while providing wisdom and reasoning for rational thinking in faith.
